HOW TO PLAY

  1. Step-1: Click on play game(Button) to play the game:"Shoot Out Emoji"
  2. Step-2: Click on New Game to start .

  3. You can change the difficulty level as per your choice by clicking the dropdown given below.
    • Easy
    • Normal
    • Hard
    • Extreme
    • Aag!

  4. You are ready to play: Now just point the emoji by single/double click to shoot the emoji.

  5. You can pause the game using "Pause" button .

  6. You can change the theme of the game by clicking on the Change Theme button at the bottom.
